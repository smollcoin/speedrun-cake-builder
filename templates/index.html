    Preparing the batter

    In a bowl, combine
    • 2 eggs
    • 100ml milk
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 120g sugar
    • 120g self-raising flour
    • 30g cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • Melted butter

    Bake

    • Pour batter into prepared tin
    • Bake for 25-35 min at 180°C

    Cool

    • Cool in tin for 5 minutes
